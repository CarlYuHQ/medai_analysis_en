- **Change**: Renamed "Double Bind Theory" to "Double Bind Framework"

- **Reason**: Eagly & Karau (2002) uses "role congruity theory", not "double bind theory". The added sentence clarifies how this study conceptualizes the double bind based on role congruity theory.

#### 1.4 Femonationalism → Femonationalism Framework
- **Change**: Renamed "Femonationalism" to "Femonationalism Framework"
- **Reason**: Consistent naming convention across all four frameworks.

#### 1.5 Intersectionality: Crenshaw 1989 → 1991
